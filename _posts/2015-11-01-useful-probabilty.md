## Moment Generating Function

Given random variable \\(X\\), its moment generating function is defined as,

$$
g(t) = E(e^{tX}),
$$

**Property**: 

$$
\frac{d^n}{d t^n}g(t)|_{t=0} = \mu_n,
$$

where \\(\mu_n\\) is the order n moment.
