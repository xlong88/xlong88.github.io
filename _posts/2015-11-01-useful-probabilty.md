## Stirling's Formula

\\(n!=\sqrt{2\pi n}\left(\frac{n}{e}\right)^n\left(1 + \frac{1}{12n} + O\left(\frac{1}{n^2}\right)\right)\\)

## Useful Inequalities

1. \\(\binom{n}{k}\le \frac{n^k}{k!}\\)
2. \\(\binom{n}{k}\le \left(\frac{ne}{k}\right)^k\\)
3. \\(\binom{n}{k} \ge \left(\frac{n}{k}\right)^k\\)
