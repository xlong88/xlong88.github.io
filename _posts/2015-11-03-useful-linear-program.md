## Primal \& Dual

1. Form 1:
**Primal**:

$$\begin{align}max.&\quad\mathbf{c}^T \mathbf{x}&\\
s.t.&&\\
&\mathbf{A}\mathbf{x} \le \mathbf{b}&\end{align}
$$

**Dual**:

$$
\begin{align}min.&\quad\mathbf{b}^T\mathbf{x}&\\
s.t.&&\\
&\mathbf{A}^T\mathbf{x}=\mathbf{c}^T&\\
&\mathbf{x}\ge \mathbf{0}&\end{align}
$$
